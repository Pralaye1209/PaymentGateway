About

The purpose of the Sparks Foundation for Education in India was to promote education among the underprivileged. Their development program includes Education, health, livelihood for children and women, provision to be equally affected by lack of resources. Some of his programs are Smile on Wheels, Mission Education and Smile Twin e-learning programs.
